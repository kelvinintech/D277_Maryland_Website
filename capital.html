About Annapolis

Annapolis, the capital city of Maryland, is rich in history and charm.

  • Population: 39,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1708
  • Region: Central Maryland
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: Higher than state average

For more information about Annapolis, visit City of Annapolis.

  1. Visit the historic Naval Academy.
  2. Explore the beautiful waterfront.
  3. Take a tour of the Maryland State House.
Year Event Significance
1708 Incorporation Annapolis was officially incorporated.
1783 Treaty of Paris The U.S. Congress ratified the Treaty of Paris in Annapolis.
1845 Naval Academy The U.S. Naval Academy was founded in Annapolis.

Fun Fact:

Annapolis is known as the "Sailing Capital of the U.S." due to its maritime heritage and thriving boating community.
