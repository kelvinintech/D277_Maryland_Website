About Bowie

Bowie, located in Prince George's County, Maryland, is a vibrant suburban city with a rich history.

  • Population: 58,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1916
  • Region: Prince George's County
  • Classification: Suburban
  • Average Income: Higher than the state average
  1. Visit the Bowie Train Museum.
  2. Explore the Allen Pond Park.
  3. Attend events at the Bowie Performing Arts Center.
Year Event Significance
1870 Founding Bowie was established as a small railroad town.
1916 Incorporation Bowie was officially incorporated as a city.
1960 Suburban Growth Bowie expanded significantly due to suburban development.

Fun Fact:

Bowie is home to the largest indoor ice skating arena in Maryland, the Bowie Ice Arena.
