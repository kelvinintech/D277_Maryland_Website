About Frederick

Frederick, located in Western Maryland, is known for its suburban feel and rich history.

  • Population: 70,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1817
  • Region: Western Maryland
  • Classification: Suburban
  • Average Income: Comparable to the state average
  1. Visit the National Museum of Civil War Medicine.
  2. Explore the historic downtown district.
  3. Enjoy the scenic Monocacy River.
Year Event Significance
1745 Founding Frederick was founded as a settlement.
1817 Incorporation Frederick was officially incorporated as a city.
1862 Civil War Battle Frederick played a key role in the Civil War.

Fun Fact:

Frederick is home to the largest collection of covered bridges in Maryland.
